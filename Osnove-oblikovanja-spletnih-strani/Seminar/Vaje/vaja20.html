Internet Usage

The table below shows the top eight types of applications favored by the approximately 144 million Internet users for 2005. E-mail is still the most used Internet application, but search engine usage is rapidly gaining, according to new data from the Pew Internet & American Life Project and comScore Media Metrix. 
            
                Internet Usage by Category
                (November 2005)
            -------------------------------------------------
                                         Users          Users
            Rank  Usage Type        (millions)      (percent)
            
            -------------------------------------------------
            1     Using email             75.0          52.0%
            2     Obtaining news          66.0          46.0%
            3     Using search engines    59.0          41.0%
            4     Job searching           42.0          29.0%
            5     Instant messaging       26.0          18.0%
            6     Online banking          26.0          18.0%
            7     Reading blogs            4.0           3.0%
            8     Online auctions          4.0           3.0%
